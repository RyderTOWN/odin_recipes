Guacamole

Descriptions

Fresh avocado mixed with lime, tomatoes, cilantro and onions

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados, peeled and pitted.
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes.
  • ¼ cup chopped onion.
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice.
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste.
  • Jalapeño

Steps

  1. Get all ingredients together
  2. Slice the avocados in half, take out the pit, and use the fork to scrape all the flesh into the bowl.
  3. Squeeze a half a lime or lemon over the avocados.
  4. Mince your garlic and dump it into the bowl.
  5. Cut your tomato in half, and scoop out all the watery bits and seeds.
  6. Cut in half, remove seeds/membrane if you want (I'd recommend leaving at least half of the jalapeno intact) and cut it into a small dice.
  7. Dice up 1/4 of your red onion finely and drop that in.
  8. Take your fork and mash mash mash, making sure everything gets well combined.
  9. Add in some pepper and good pinches of salt and chili powder, and mix and taste.
